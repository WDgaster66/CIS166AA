HOA Schedule

Welcome to the Higley Manor HOA calendar! Here you can find all of our upcoming events and important dates. We encourage all of our residents to get involved and attend as many events as possible. We look forward to seeing you there!
May 2023
- May 7th, 10:00am - 12:00pm: Community Garage Sale - Come browse the selection of goods for sale from Higley Manor residents. The garage sale will be held at the community center parking lot.
- May 14th, 6:00pm - 8:00pm: HOA Board Meeting - This is an open meeting for all Higley Manor residents. We will discuss upcoming events, community issues, and more. The meeting will be held at the community center.
- May 28th, 12:00pm - 4:00pm: Memorial Day Picnic - Join us for a fun-filled afternoon with food, games, and activities. The picnic will be held at the Higley Manor park.
June 2023
- June 11th, 9:00am - 11:00am: Community Clean-Up Day - Let's work together to keep our community looking great! Meet us at the community center to help with litter pickup, weeding, and other outdoor projects.
- June 18th, 6:00pm - 9:00pm: Summer Block Party - Bring your lawn chairs and join us for an evening of food, music, and fun. The block party will be held on Maple Street.
- June 25th, 7:00pm - 10:00pm: Outdoor Movie Night - We will be showing a family-friendly movie on the lawn at the Higley Manor park. Bring your blankets and chairs for a cozy movie night under the stars.

Thank you for visiting our HOA calendar page. We hope to see you at our upcoming events! If you have any questions or suggestions, please don't hesitate to contact us.
